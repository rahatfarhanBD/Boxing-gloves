About Boxing Gloves

Boxing gloves help to protect the hands when kicking pads or heavy bags, while minimizing impact and injuries for yourself and training partners in sparring. However, boxing gloves come in different materials, weights, designs for different occasions and sports.


Source: Yokkao

4 Main Types of Boxing Gloves:

  1. Training Gloves
  2. Bag Gloves
  3. Sparring Gloves
  4. Competetion Gloves
